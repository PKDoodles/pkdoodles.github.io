Potion Commotion

A calm, cosy (but sometimes chaotic) potion brewing game! Combine magical ingredients to create potions for customers and earn money! Make enough to pay rent, or the landlord won't be happy! Filled with unique minigames and morally ambiguous concoctions of the arcane. Meet the quota, or get evicted trying!

Project Overview

For this project, I lead a group of 27 people to make a fully polished over the course of 8 weeks! As producer, I constantly worked on the game: resovling people's questions and conflicts; managing and organising the team to maintain scope; and making sure the vision remains clear for everyone working on the game (with so much more)!

This was my first every role as producer and it taught me so much about what it actually takes to make a game! There were so many challenges I overcame and I felt I did an amazing job fitting into the leadership role. By the end, my team all thought very highly of me and I am so proud of what we were able to achieve by the end of it!
