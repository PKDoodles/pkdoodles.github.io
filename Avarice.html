Project Overview

Avarice was a story and world I created for my Narrative Design University Module where I was tasked with creating a story-driven game. Avarice is a mystery game based in a city of Avarice, a industrial city that has excelled it's development thanks to a new magical ore known as Avaritum. However, a mysterious plague has overrun the city in which the Doctor (you) must solve the mysteries afoot!

This project was my first true introduction to storytelling and I feel I achieved an exhilierating and in-depth story with meaningful decisions and a convincing mystery! I learnt a lot about the importance of pacing and the importance of intergrating character flaws, motivations and connections to characters to develop realism and relatability!
