Project Overview

Here I made a mixture of a comedy-shooter based around knockback where the player is tasked with infiltrating a museum! Here I really leant into the environemental story telling and created a very uniqie interpretation of a very sci-fi based framework! This was developed using the "Lancer" framwwork for my university module.

This project taught me a very important lesson about work effort as I initially had a very rough time with this module and gradually changed my schedule to be healthier which has improved my mentality towards games even more! I also learnt a lot about the importance of pacing and visualisation in GDDs to assist in conveying ideas to others!
