Project Overview

Steam Funk is another University Project in which I was tasked on developing a level focused on developing more advanced and intruiging mechanics around a top-down tank game the framework providede. I decided to go the extra mile and create a rhythm game out of it!

I created many different Enemies, Weapons, Materials and Niagara Effects using important Game Development Principals such as inheritance. This greatly expanded my Unreal Engine skills in both mechanical design and visual appeal. I also used sequencers to create a rhythm mechanic and cutscene to develop it far beyond the generic framework!

I feel I learnt so much from this module and greatly improved from my first project! During the development of the project, I had to cut down on the scope of the project and I had a lot of issues developing the rhythm mechanic. However with the help of both researching online and the amazing lecturers, I was able to create a level I am proud of!
