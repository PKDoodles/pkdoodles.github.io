Project Overview

Here is all my animation work I have achieved! Most of this is using Maya and keyframe animation to solidify my fundamentals of animations!

I feel I have come a long way from my inital animation and utilised core prinicipals such as slow in and slow out very effectively to create emotive and exciting animations!

As I animate more, I want to put more focus into appeal, follow through and anticipation to add more naturality to actions. My animation is just a hobby but I am always looking to improve if the opportunity arrives!
