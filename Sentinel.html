Project Overview

For this project I designed and created a level for my University Module 'Intro to Games Design'. I created a light horror-platformer level with a core mechanic of avoiding the darkness! This was developed from the University Framework 'Sentinel' which provided basic movement and assets.

This project has taught me a lot of lessons about the Games Industry and being a Game Designer such as:

⦿ The importance of lighting affordance to guide players and highlight core objectives
⦿ The vitality of testing and iteration to improve the quality of a game greatly beyond the designers views
⦿ The impact camera angles and subtle 'juice' can have on the overall atmosphere of an area
