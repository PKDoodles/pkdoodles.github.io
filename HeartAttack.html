Project Overview

Heart Attack was the first ever time I worked in Unity and was a small project where I explored and utilised the many different features of Unity to get a basic grasp of the Engine! This was also my first time using C#!

The game is a very simple wave fighter where the player's health is tied to a floating Heart which they can also launch and hit to cause "Heart Attacks!" I really liked this concept as it was based off an old game concept I made that made me a finalist in the BAFTA Young Game Designers Competition!

Learning the Unity Engine has been a huge milestone in my Game Design career as now I have been able to take it further and utilise it in future projects. I thoroughly enjoyed this project and feel it turned out well!
