Project Overview

Mainsring is a University Project focused on the pre-production of Game Design in which we were tasked with creating a Design Doc of a unique game concept. My game concept was called Mainspring: a isometric action game based on clockwork and purpose!

This project allowed me to expand my scope to create a dream game of mine while teaching me about the many important considerations a game has before even reaching the engine. Mainspring is the most fleshed-out concept I have ever made which may inspire me to attempt to create in the future (once my development skills have improved)!
Mainspring has taught me many things:
⦿ How to develop a proffesional design document and what to consider
⦿ The importance of a gameplay loop and core design pillars to keep the game grounded and in-scope
⦿ How impactful accessibility and pricing can be when designing the game and it's concepts
⦿ How to pitch a game with confidence, what to highlight, and what to avoid
