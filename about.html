Hello!
I'm Luke Pierson, an aspiring student studying Game Design and Production at Staffordshire University. I have great passion for creating games that weave narratives, mechanics and environments into brilliant gameplay and amazing experiences!

I mostly focus on narrative, pre-production and visual scripting as I have a love for creating more experimental and innovative mechanics and stories. I also posees skills in scripting, level Design and systems / mechanics design.

My favourite game is Tunic, the brilliance of the games knowledge-based progression as well as the overall beauty in the instruction book combined with intricate puzzles and combat make it a game everyone should experience! I wish I could play it again for the first time!

Beyond video games I am a huge board game fanatic owning well over 20 games and play tabletop RPGs! I also love to travel and have been very fortunate to visit all sorts of places in my lifetime. My top trip is when I backpacked parts of Europe!
