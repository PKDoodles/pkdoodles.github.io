Project Overview

"Tank Game!" was a collaborative game between myself and another person in which we aimed to make a game that utilised a unique controller scheme and fun gameplay interaction!

This project game me a lot of creative control and I am extremely pleased with the outcome! I put a lot of my previous skills to the test as well as taught me a whole new discipline of robotics and utilising bespoke controllers such as VR and Arduinos!
This was also my first collaborative project which taught me how important showing clarity in the ideas presented; how to make sure each teammate is working evenly and share the same vision!
